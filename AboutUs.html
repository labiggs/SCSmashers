About Us

Welcome to the home of the SC SMASHERS, South Carolina's pride in Major League Baseball! Founded with a passion for the game and a commitment to excellence, we are more than just a team; we are a symbol of unity and sportsmanship.

Our journey began with a vision to bring top-tier baseball to the heart of South Carolina, and today, we stand as a testament to the dedication of our players, coaching staff, and loyal fans. We strive to embody the spirit of the Palmetto State in every inning, both on and off the field.

The SC SMASHERS organization is built on a foundation of integrity, teamwork, and community. From the front office to the dugout, our leadership is committed to fostering a culture of excellence. Our coaching staff brings a wealth of experience and expertise, guiding our players to reach their full potential both as athletes and individuals.

Off the field, we are deeply invested in the community that has embraced us. Through various outreach programs and events, we actively engage with fans, support local initiatives, and foster a love for the game among the youth. Our commitment extends beyond the ballpark, as we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of those we touch.

As we chase victories and pursue excellence in the league, we invite you to join us on this thrilling journey. Whether you're a seasoned baseball enthusiast or a newcomer to the sport, there's a place for you in the SC SMASHERS family. Let's celebrate the passion for baseball and the vibrant spirit of South Carolina together!
